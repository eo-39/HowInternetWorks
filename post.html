How The Internet Works

Posted by Ebinehita Olumese on October 7, 2020

Previously we learned about the History of the internet! The internet evolved from being old large computers in a room to cloud/ mini devices that range across the entire world! Sites like Amazon, Facebook, eBay, and Craiglist dominating the market and drawing a mass number of people towards the internet! After, reading about this history one would wonder just how does the internet work? How do websites create webpages? How do users access them? What allows us to enter important information without it getting stolen? In this project, we touch on those topics that explain everything we need to know about the Internet and what makes it work

"The Internet works because a lot of people cooperate to do things together."
