Domain and ICANN
By Ebinehita Olumese

The first topic we talk about is Domain names and ICANN. A domain name is an address where Internet users can access your website. The domain is comprised of two elements, which is before and after the “.” so .com,.net,etc.. Are the top level domain and the information to the left is the domain name that must be registered. Through different, you can pay to acquire your domain name

ICANN which stands for Internet Corporation For Assigned Names and Numbers, helps deal with domain name system management. Formed in 1998, This not for profit partnership all over the world is dedicated to keeping the internet secure. How does ICANN work with domain names? it manages name system management and has contractual agreements with both registries and registrars that provide the foundation for the WHOIS system
